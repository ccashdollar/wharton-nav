Child Theme Prototype

Dr. Bruce I. Jacobs — Master of Science in Quantitative Finance

This is a functional prototype demonstrating the child-level navigation for a Wharton sub-site. The child theme uses a dark identity bar with the program name alongside the Wharton logo, while maintaining the same universal and primary navigation structure.

Key differences from parent theme:

• Dark identity bar with white Wharton logo + child program name
• Custom primary nav items specific to this program
• Same responsive behavior and mobile menu pattern
• "More..." collects overflow items that don't fit the horizontal nav

This pattern works for any child installation — each child site provides its own nav items and program name; everything else is inherited from the shared navigation framework.

About

For over a century, Wharton has been at the forefront of business education, producing leaders who shape industries and drive meaningful change. Our commitment to rigorous academic inquiry, combined with real-world application, prepares students for the challenges of tomorrow.

With a global network of alumni spanning every continent and virtually every industry, the Wharton community continues to grow and evolve, fostering connections that last a lifetime.

Our faculty includes world-renowned researchers, thought leaders, and practitioners who bring diverse perspectives to the classroom. Through cutting-edge research and innovative teaching methods, they inspire students to think critically and act boldly.

Whether you are a prospective student, current member of the community, or alumni looking to stay connected, we invite you to explore all that Wharton has to offer.
